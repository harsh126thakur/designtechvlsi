Signals and Systems

This course explains the fundamental concepts of signals and systems which are essential for electronics, communication, and VLSI engineers. The course focuses on building strong conceptual understanding required for interviews and advanced engineering subjects.

Topics Covered

  • Introduction to Signals
  • Continuous-Time and Discrete-Time Signals
  • System Properties
  • Linear Time Invariant Systems
  • Convolution
  • Fourier Series
  • Fourier Transform
  • Laplace Transform
  • Z Transform
  • System Stability and Causality

Course Benefits

  • Strong foundation for communication and DSP
  • Important concepts for GATE and interviews
  • Clear mathematical understanding
  • Conceptual learning with practical examples

Who Should Take This Course

  • ECE Students
  • Students preparing for GATE
  • Beginners learning signal processing
  • Engineers preparing for technical interviews
