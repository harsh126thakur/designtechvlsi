Harsh Raj Thakur

Harsh Raj Thakur is the founder of DesignTech VLSI and a passionate mentor in semiconductor education. He has qualified GATE six times with strong ranks and completed two master’s degrees: High Voltage Engineering from NIT Calicut and VLSI from NIT Meghalaya. During his academic career he served as Placement Coordinator at NIT Meghalaya, helping students secure opportunities in industry. He also co-founded the NITIANS Deep Learning Network and has mentored more than 5000 students in electronics, machine learning, and semiconductor design. His industry exposure includes project work and training with Maven Silicon on practical VLSI design flows. Through DesignTech VLSI he aims to simplify semiconductor education and guide students towards careers in ASIC design, research, and advanced electronics.
