Harsh Raj Thakur

Harsh Raj Thakur is the founder of DesignTech VLSI and a mentor dedicated to semiconductor education. He has qualified GATE six times and completed two master's degrees: High Voltage Engineering from NIT Calicut and VLSI from NIT Meghalaya. He served as Placement Coordinator at NIT Meghalaya and has mentored more than 5000 students in electronics and semiconductor design. He also co-founded the NITIANS Deep Learning Network and gained industry exposure through VLSI training and projects with Maven Silicon. Through DesignTech VLSI he aims to simplify advanced semiconductor concepts and guide students toward careers in ASIC design and research.
