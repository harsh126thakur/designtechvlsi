Harsh Raj Thakur

Harsh Raj Thakur is a Research Scholar at IIT Roorkee working in the area of VLSI architectures, semiconductor systems, and digital design methodologies. His research focuses on efficient hardware architectures and advanced chip design techniques used in modern semiconductor industries.

He has more than 8 years of teaching and mentoring experience and has worked with multiple EdTech platforms and semiconductor industry initiatives. Over the years, he has mentored and guided 5000+ students in electronics, VLSI design, and semiconductor career preparation.

He has also contributed to technical training initiatives and collaborative programs, including work associated with Assam Rifles (DGAR), Meghalaya. In addition, he is the Co-Founder of the NITIANS Deep Learning Network, a community focused on research, innovation, and knowledge sharing in artificial intelligence and advanced computing technologies.

As the founder of DesignTech VLSI, his mission is to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry requirements by providing structured courses, practical VLSI training, and mentorship to students aspiring to build careers in the semiconductor industry.
