❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is VLSI? +

VLSI involves designing integrated circuits by combining millions of transistors into a single chip.

Is VLSI a good career in 2026? +

Yes, VLSI is a high-demand field with strong growth in semiconductor industries globally.

What skills are required? +

Digital design, Verilog/SystemVerilog, CMOS concepts, and problem-solving are key skills.

Can beginners start VLSI? +

Yes, with structured learning and mentorship, beginners can enter VLSI successfully.
