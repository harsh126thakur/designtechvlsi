Harsh Raj Thakur

Harsh Raj Thakur is the founder of DesignTech VLSI and an educator dedicated to guiding students towards careers in semiconductor engineering and advanced electronics research. He has qualified the Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering (GATE) six times with strong ranks, reflecting deep academic strength in core electronics. He completed two master's degrees: High Voltage Engineering from NIT Calicut and VLSI Engineering from NIT Meghalaya. During his academic journey he served as Placement Coordinator at NIT Meghalaya, actively helping students connect with industry opportunities. Harsh also co-founded the NITIANS Deep Learning Network and has mentored more than 5000 students in electronics, machine learning, and semiconductor design. His industry exposure includes training and project experience with Maven Silicon, where he worked on live VLSI design flows. Earlier in his career he received the Best Project Award during his B.Tech studies in Tamil Nadu. Through DesignTech VLSI he focuses on simplifying complex VLSI concepts and helping students prepare for research careers, semiconductor jobs, and competitive examinations like GATE.
