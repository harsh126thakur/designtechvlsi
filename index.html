Harsh Raj Thakur

Harsh Raj Thakur is the founder of DesignTech VLSI and an educator dedicated to helping students build strong careers in semiconductor engineering. He has qualified the Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering (GATE) six times with strong ranks and completed two master's degrees: High Voltage Engineering from NIT Calicut and VLSI Engineering from NIT Meghalaya. During his academic career he served as Placement Coordinator at NIT Meghalaya and actively helped students connect with industry opportunities. He also co-founded the NITIANS Deep Learning Network and has mentored more than 5000 students in electronics, machine learning, and semiconductor design. His industry exposure includes training and project experience with Maven Silicon where he worked on practical VLSI design flows. Through DesignTech VLSI he focuses on simplifying complex semiconductor concepts and guiding students toward careers in ASIC design, research, and advanced electronics.
