Network Theory

This course builds a strong foundation in electrical network analysis, an essential subject for electronics engineers and VLSI students. The course focuses on circuit analysis techniques, theorems, and system understanding required for interviews and competitive exams.

Topics Covered

  • Basic Circuit Elements
  • Ohm's Law and Kirchhoff’s Laws
  • Node Voltage Method
  • Mesh Current Method
  • Superposition Theorem
  • Thevenin and Norton Theorems
  • Maximum Power Transfer
  • Transient Analysis
  • Two-Port Networks
  • Frequency Response of Circuits

Course Benefits

  • Strong circuit analysis foundation
  • Important concepts for GATE and technical interviews
  • Better understanding of electronic circuits
  • Preparation for advanced electronics subjects

Who Should Take This Course

  • ECE Students
  • Students preparing for GATE
  • Beginners learning circuit analysis
  • Engineers preparing for technical interviews
