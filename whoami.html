Harsh Raj Thakur

Research Scholar • VLSI Engineer • Educator

Harsh Raj Thakur is currently a Research Scholar at the Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee (IIT Roorkee) , working in semiconductor design and VLSI systems. He is also the founder of DesignTech VLSI, an educational platform dedicated to teaching VLSI design, semiconductor technologies, and EDA tools.

He has qualified the GATE examination six times and completed two master's degrees: • M.Tech in High Voltage Engineering — NIT Calicut • M.Tech in VLSI Design — NIT Meghalaya

He has mentored more than 5000 students in semiconductor design, VLSI architectures, and electronic design automation tools.
