Harsh Raj Thakur

Research Scholar • VLSI Engineer • Educator

About

Harsh Raj Thakur is a research scholar and the founder of DesignTech VLSI, a platform dedicated to semiconductor education and VLSI training. His work focuses on bridging the gap between theoretical academic knowledge and practical semiconductor industry skills.

He has qualified the GATE examination six times and completed two master's degrees: • M.Tech in High Voltage Engineering — NIT Calicut • M.Tech in VLSI Design — NIT Meghalaya

He has mentored more than 5000 students in VLSI design, semiconductor fundamentals, and EDA tools.
