Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about DesignTech VLSI courses

Do I need prior VLSI knowledge to take these courses?
No. The courses start from fundamental concepts and gradually move towards advanced semiconductor design topics.
Are industry tools covered in the courses?
Yes. The courses introduce important Electronic Design Automation (EDA) tools used in the semiconductor industry.
Will these courses help me prepare for semiconductor jobs?
Yes. The training focuses on practical understanding of VLSI design flows, which are essential for semiconductor industry roles.
Do you provide mentorship or guidance?
Yes. Personal mentorship sessions are available where students can receive guidance on VLSI careers and research.
Who should take these courses?
These courses are ideal for ECE students, researchers, and professionals who want to build a career in semiconductor design.
Is there a refund policy?
Yes. We provide a 7-day money back guarantee if you are not satisfied with the course.
